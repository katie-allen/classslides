## 7 methods of encoding data

#### Ordered by accuracy of perceptual estimation.

1. Position along a common scale
2. Position on common, non-aligned scale
3. Length
4. Angle or slope
5. Area
6. Volume/density/saturation of "color"
7. Color hue
